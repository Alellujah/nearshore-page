How it works

IT isn't easy to manage. You can have ten of equipments to manage or ten thousands of them. Now imagine you need to install Acrobat Reader on everyone of them, how do you do it?

Pile up ten thousand of PC's on your desk and start installing one by one?

We've got a better solution. You just name what application you want to install, with whatever parameters or configurations, that suits you the most and then we create your package. You deploy it easily with one click.

Four simple steps
  1. Pick the application
  2. Choose your desired parameters or configurations
  3. We'll deliver a fully optimal package
  4. You deploy it silently and without any fuzz

Saving troubles on short and long term.

Deploy

We will give you a package that's technology agnostic. SCCM*, Landesk*, you call in which to use.

Update

Forget about issuing users to update. They won't do it. You can update your package with a single click, as easy as deploying it.

Stay Safe

Users won't have to insert serial keys, they won't see any software ad. You'll be in charge and they'll be safe.

1 click to rule them all

1 click to install, 1 click to update, 1 click to uninstall. Manage your company IT in a simple and time saving way.

*SCCM and Landesk are tools to ease deploying on multiple computers. It's a different scope, but you can ask us about it, we'll gladly help you.
